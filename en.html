As of July 1, 2025, Vietnam has merged many provinces and cities. Smaller administrative units within them have also been consolidated. Consequently, many familiar old addresses have disappeared, replaced by new and unfamiliar place names.

With this tool, tourists will no longer have to worry about finding the addresses of tourist destinations in Vietnam.
